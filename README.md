# Pig-Game

Welcome to the classic Pig Dice Game! This game allows two players to engage in some exciting dice-rolling action, all on the same computer.
Description

The Pig Dice Game is a timeless classic that's easy to pick up and play. Roll the dice, accumulate points, and strive to be the first player to reach a total score of 100 points to emerge victorious.
How to Play
Objective

# Main Goal
  The main goal of the game is to reach a total score of 100 points to secure your victory!


# Game Controls

You'll find three main buttons to control the game:

1. New Game
    Feel free to restart the game anytime you like by clicking the "New Game" button.

2. Roll Dice
    The "Roll Dice" button is where the excitement happens. Click it as many times as you want during your turn.
    With each roll, the value of the dice will be added to your current score.
    But beware, if you roll a 1, you'll lose all your current score, and your turn will swiftly pass to the next player.
    To safeguard your progress, click the "Hold" button to add your current score to your total score.

3. Hold
    Safely store your current score by pressing the "Hold" button. All those hard-earned points will be added to your total score.
    As an added bonus, the turn will automatically pass to the next player.

Now that you know the rules and controls, it's time to enjoy some Pig Dice Game action! Good luck, and may the best roller win!
